2DMapBuilder

2DMapBuilder is a 2D map editor that helps you develop the scenario for your 2D games. Its primary focus is to aid map designers in tile map editing, as well as sprites and map objects arranging. 2DMapBuilder was used in other projects such as "Pucmon". Access the project's wiki for more details.

Technology: Java, Swing, XML, Eclipse
