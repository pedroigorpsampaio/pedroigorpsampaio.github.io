Mice Challenge

Mice Challenge is an experimental game used as a tool for our researches at ICAD/VisionLab in PUC-Rio. It helped ongoing researches and provided the means of experimenting for two publications that contributed in the Artificial Intelligence (AI) and in the Procedural Content Generation (PCG) fields of informatics. See the publications for more details.

Technology: C#, MonoGame, Visual Studio.
