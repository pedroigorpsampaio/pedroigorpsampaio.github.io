Pucmon

Pucmon is the result of a collaboration between LAC and ICAD/VisionLab (Laboratory of Visualization, Digital TV/Cinema, and Games) from PUC-Rio. This game prototype explores ways of increasing the experience of players through pervasive mechanics while taking advantage of the player’s motivation to perform sensing tasks. It also incorporates serious applications into the gameplay, such as the localization of facilities and services. Download the project's .pdf for more details.

Technology: Java, LibGDX, ContextNet, MySQL, phpMyAdmin, XAMPP, AndroidStudio, Gradle.
