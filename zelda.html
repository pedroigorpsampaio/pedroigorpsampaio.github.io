Zelda AI

A knowledge based agent capable of reason in an unknown environment based on the world of The Legend of Zelda games. The agent starts with no knowledge of the environment around him, and must gather information through sensors that can detect near events. The goal of the agent is to find the Master Sword that is located somewhere in the environment while maintaining the effort to the lowest. To provide a visualization of the agent in action, a graphic interface was implemented with the art of Nintendo's The Legend of Zelda: A Link to the Past game. See media for videos and pictures. For the complete explanation of the rules, download the .pptx or the .pdf. Alternatively, the main video is also coupled with the rules, and there is a special Rules section for this project that provides a slideshow with the rules. You can also download the .prolog code to better understand the strategy that was used in order to complete the agent goal.

Technology: Java, Swing, Prolog, SWI-Prolog, Eclipse
